Alarm System

Safe and sound ▼

The house is secured by a modern alarm system. We invite you to use it, although the area is known for its general safety.

Please note: False alarms may result in a police response.

The 3 Modes

Unsecured
Select when you are at home and want to move freely inside the house and in the outdoor areas. No alarm will be triggered.
Secured exterior
Select when everyone leaves the house. The alarm will be triggered by open doors/windows and by motion detectors inside the house.
Secured interior
Select for the night. Alarm only when doors/windows are opened — no motion sensors. You can move freely inside the house.

Operation

Turn off alarm (unsecured) Place the key fob in the centre of the panel until you hear a beep. On return: 30 seconds to deactivate.
Activate exterior mode Close all doors, shutters and windows. Press button (1) and leave the house within 30 seconds.
Activate interior mode Close all doors, shutters and windows. Press button (2).
